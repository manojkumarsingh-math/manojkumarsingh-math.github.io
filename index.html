Dr. Manoj Kumar Singh

Assistant Professor of Mathematics

Department of Mathematics, St. Xavier's College, Ranchi, Jharkhand — 834001, India

I am an Assistant Professor of Mathematics at St. Xavier's College, Ranchi, with a Ph.D. from IIT(ISM) Dhanbad (2016). My research spans Functional Analysis, Pseudo-differential Operators, Wavelet and Fourier Transform theory, and their applications to Machine Learning. I am particularly interested in pseudo-differential operators associated with Jacobi differential operators and fractional transforms, as well as the intersection of classical harmonic analysis with modern data-driven methods.

Ph.D. IIT(ISM) Dhanbad, 2016 M.Sc. University of Hyderabad, 2011 B.Sc. St. Xavier's College, Ranchi, 2009
